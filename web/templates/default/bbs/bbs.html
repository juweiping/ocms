论坛新贴(不带详细内容):
[@dz.newthread return="list"/]
    [#list list as thread]
  • [${thread['name']}]${thread['subject']}
    • [/#list]
论坛新贴(带详细内容):
[@dz.newthread return="list" detail=1/]
    [#list list as thread]
  • [${thread['name']}]${thread['subject']}

    • 详细内容：${dzUtil.getMessageText(thread['message']!'')} [/#list]

论坛精华贴方式2,调用板块id为98的精华3级别的帖子:
[@dz.digest2 return="list" nums="15" level="3" fid="98"/]
    [#list list as thread]
  • [${thread['name']}][@util.substr target=thread['subject'] start=0 limit=20/]
    • [/#list]
